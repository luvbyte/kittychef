KittyChef — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

1. Overview

KittyChef respects your privacy. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how your information is protected.

2. No Data Collection

KittyChef does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All input and output processing happens locally on your device.

3. No Account Required

KittyChef does not require you to create an account or share personal information.

4. No Cookies or Trackers

KittyChef does not use:

  • Cookies
  • Analytics trackers
  • Fingerprinting technologies
  • Any form of personal tracking

5. Local-Only Processing

All text transformation, encoding, decoding, and cryptographic operations are executed directly in your browser using JavaScript. Your data never leaves your device.

6. Third-Party Services

KittyChef does not share your information with third parties. If the app is hosted on a platform (e.g., Vercel, Netlify, GitHub Pages), those providers may have their own server logs, but KittyChef does not send them your data.

7. Security

Although no system is 100% secure, KittyChef minimizes risk by:

  • Not storing or transmitting data
  • Running fully client-side
  • Avoiding unnecessary permissions

8. Children's Privacy

KittyChef does not target children and does not collect personal data from any users.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page.

10. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us via the KittyChef website.
